About Me

Ademuyiwa Adewuyi is a Front-End developer based in Lagos, Nigeria.

I am passionate about bringing the technical and aesthetic visual facets of digital products to life. As a passionate web developer, I care deeply about optimum user experience, web accessibility and beautiful designs. Equally important to me is writing clean code that is not only machine understable, but also human readable.
In addition, I am a proponent of minimal design. I wouldn't trade web accessibility or great user experience for overbloated web designs/apps.

You can be sure when you work with me you will be getting every penny of your money's worth. From the design to the coding phase, I put in my all. I pay great attention to every aspect of bringing your product(s) to life. An assertion upon which my wonderful track record is built, and which further serves as a lingering testament. It's a feeling I strongly relish. After all, coding for me is life.
